Add Serial Unit
The Add Serial Unit popup will remain open after adding a new unit. This allows you to easily add multiple units in a row. Click the X in the top-right corner or Cancel when you are finished.

Stencil-only: click Serial Number, then node src/cli.js -n CAM -c 5 -l 100 --countdown 3 (Tabs ×3 → Stencil → Enter).
With serial: node src/cli.js -n CAM -c 5 -l 100 --with-serial — type Serial here, press Enter in the terminal to fill Stencil + ADD.
